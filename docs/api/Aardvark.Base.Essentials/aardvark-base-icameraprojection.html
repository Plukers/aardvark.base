ProjectionTrafo
Signature: Trafo3d
Modifiers: abstract

Transforms the right-handed camera space (looking down the negative z-axis) to left-handed normalized device coordinates of the canonical view volume defined as [(-1, -1, 0), (+1, +1, +1)]. Here the x-axis goes to the right, the y-axis goes up, and the z-axis goes into the viewport, with near plane mapped to z = 0 and far plane to z = 1.
